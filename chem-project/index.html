About Our School

Alymbek Datka Lyceum is one of the leading educational institutions in Kyrgyzstan, focused on modern science education with an emphasis on analytical thinking and innovation.

💡 Did You Know?

⚛️

Your Body is Chemistry

The human body contains about 60 chemical elements. Oxygen, carbon, hydrogen, and nitrogen make up over 96% of your body mass.

💧

Water is Weird

Water is one of the only substances that expands when it freezes. This is why ice floats — and why life in frozen lakes can survive winter!

🌟

Stars Make Elements

Every atom heavier than hydrogen in your body was forged inside a star that exploded billions of years ago. You are literally made of stardust.

🔋

Electrochemistry Powers Us

Every message your neurons send, every heartbeat — powered by electrochemical reactions with sodium, potassium, and calcium ions.

🌟 Inspiring Chemists

Marie Curie

"Nothing in life is to be feared, only to be understood." — First woman to win a Nobel Prize. Discovered polonium and radium.

Dmitri Mendeleev

Created the Periodic Table of Elements in 1869. He even predicted the properties of elements not yet discovered!

Linus Pauling

Won TWO Nobel Prizes — Chemistry AND Peace. His work on chemical bonding transformed modern chemistry.
